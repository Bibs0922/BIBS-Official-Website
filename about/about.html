ABOUT BIBS

Boston International Business School (“BIBS” as below) was established in 2020, partnered with American Weston Academy, focusing on international business education, entrepreneurship and innovation.

BIBS aims to provide a new generation of cutting-edge business education, and eyes on the deep change in the world economy in the coming decades.

In 2024, BIBS started its professional and executive educational programs, lead by world-renowned faculties and dedicated to high-level executives who is interested in updating their management skills and visions to a global level.
